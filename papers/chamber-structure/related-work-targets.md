# Related Work Targets

This file lists citation targets to verify before drafting. It is not a completed bibliography.

## Representation and Chamber Design

- Bicameralism and veto points.
- Apportionment and malapportionment.
- Federal upper chambers.
- Districting and representation.
- Term length, selection, and retention mechanisms.

## Committee Systems

- Committee gatekeeping.
- Committee information theories.
- Committee distributive theories.
- Party-ratio assignment and specialization.
- Conference committees and substitute amendments.

## Review and Correction

- Judicial review and independent review.
- Sunset review and law revision.
- Oversight and administrative correction.
- Ex ante versus ex post review.

## Empirical Targets

- Congressional committee reporting data.
- Chamber composition and apportionment data.
- Voteview or comparable roll-call coalition data.
- Cross-national parliamentary and bicameralism datasets.
- District-level public opinion or constituency datasets.

## Citation Rules for the Draft

- Do not claim district representation unless the simulator output is district-level.
- Do not cite current flow sanity checks as validation of representation.
- Keep empirical sources distinct from synthetic diagnostic definitions.
