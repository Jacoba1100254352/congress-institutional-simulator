## Full Draft Go Conditions

Move to manuscript drafting only when all of the following are true:

1. The empirical-validation breakout has a source registry and expanded data beyond the current six ready source families.
2. Fairness-control scenarios compare conventional and simple-majority baselines against content-improvement mechanisms under comparable process budgets.
3. Paired-comparison reports exist for agenda, committee, veto, lobbying, and content-selection families.
4. Uncertainty reports separate seed variance from scenario variation.
5. Generator-sensitivity reports include public-benefit/support decoupling and lobbying-information worlds.
6. Claims ledger entries C3-C7 have direct report outputs and citation targets.
7. Public support, capture, harm, and correction remain labeled synthetic/proxy-only unless new empirical data is integrated.
8. The draft contribution is institutional model behavior under explicit assumptions, not the ACM framework contribution.

## Final Recommendation

Keep this as a research plan. Build validation and fairness-control evidence before drafting.
