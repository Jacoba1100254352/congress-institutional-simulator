# Claims Ledger

| Claim | Supporting evidence currently available | Limitation | Citation needed |
|---|---|---|---|
| The simulator can compare legislative mechanism bundles under shared synthetic legislatures and bill streams. | `paper/acm-ci-framework/acm-ci-framework.pdf`; `paper/technical-appendix/odd-d-appendix.pdf`; source code under `src/main/java/congresssim`. | This is already the ACM framework claim and cannot be the main novelty here. | ODD/ODD+D and agent-based legislative simulation methods. |
| Threshold-only changes do not explain observed mechanism behavior in the implemented campaign. | `reports/simulation-campaign-v21-paper.csv`; `reports/paper-findings-validation.md`; mechanism rows for `CUR`, `SM`, `DP`, `DPM`, `SEL`, `PORT`. | Confounded by proposal-transformation modules and unequal administrative budgets. | Agenda control, veto players, spatial voting, legislative bargaining. |
| Content-selection stages change the productivity/risk surface more than final passage thresholds under the baseline generator. | PAIR/AMT and selection-family summaries in `reports/paper-findings-validation.md`; full campaign CSV. | Baseline generator rewards moderation; PAIR and AMT are nearly duplicate in current results. | Social choice and amendment agenda manipulation; legislative amendment theory. |
| Burden-shifting systems are high-throughput stress cases with worse low-support/risk diagnostics. | `default-pass` and `default-pass + mediation/challenge` summaries in `reports/paper-findings-validation.md`. | These are synthetic stress tests, not proposals or empirical forecasts. | Status quo reversal, fast-track authority, default rules literature if used. |
| Portfolio variants reduce some capture/risk diagnostics but add administrative load. | `portfolio hybrid` summaries in `reports/paper-findings-validation.md`; appendix robustness tables. | Portfolio mechanism is a bundled prototype; causal attribution requires ablation and cost controls. | Institutional layering, review mechanisms, anti-capture design. |
| Empirical checks only screen flow plausibility for the conventional benchmark. | `reports/calibration-baseline.md`; `reports/empirical-bridge.csv`; `reports/empirical-validation-gap-report.md`. | Does not validate public benefit, public support, harm, capture, or revision moderation. | Voteview, Congress.gov, LDA, Comparative Agendas, empirical legislative productivity. |
| Generator assumptions materially affect the interpretation of mechanism rankings. | Generator formula in main paper and appendix; adversarial/generator cases in `reports/scenario-selection-manifest.md`. | Current alternative worlds are summarized, not yet a full independent sensitivity study. | Simulation validation and model uncertainty literature. |
