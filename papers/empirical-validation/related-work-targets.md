# Related Work Targets

This file lists citation targets to verify before drafting. It is not a completed bibliography.

## Simulation Validation

- Agent-based model calibration and validation.
- Plausibility checks versus predictive validation.
- Held-out validation in computational social science.
- Sensitivity analysis and uncertainty reporting.

## Legislative Data

- Congress.gov data documentation.
- Voteview roll-call and ideal-point documentation.
- Lobbying Disclosure Act data documentation.
- Comparative Agendas Project.
- Center for Effective Lawmaking or comparable legislative productivity data.
- V-Dem or comparative-institution indicators if used.

## Public Opinion and Representation

- District-level public opinion estimation.
- Policy congruence and representation.
- Affected-group and minority-rights measurement.

## Implementation and Correction

- Court review datasets.
- Law revision and sunset-review records.
- Policy implementation feedback.
- Administrative burden measurement.

## Citation Rules for the Draft

- Cite data documentation for source claims.
- Cite methods papers for validation terminology.
- Do not cite empirical flow data as support for synthetic welfare or harm metrics.
