Status: future-work plan only; not ready for a full manuscript draft or extended empirical paper.

The current repository has useful empirical flow sanity checks, but it does not yet have validation evidence for the paper's central synthetic quantities: public benefit, public support, revision moderation, harm, capture, administrative cost, correction over time, or policy yield.

Files in this folder:

- `paper-plan.md`: future-work decision and validation agenda.
- `claims-ledger.md`: what current evidence can and cannot support.
- `experiment-plan.md`: staged validation and data-integration plan.
- `figure-table-plan.md`: validation visuals needed in the future.
- `related-work-targets.md`: empirical and validation literature targets.
- `draft-outline.md`: future paper outline only.

No full manuscript draft was created because the current empirical layer is too thin.
