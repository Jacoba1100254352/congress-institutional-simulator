# Empirical Validation and Benchmark Data Breakout

Final decision: BENCHMARK/DATA PIPELINE PLAN ONLY. DO NOT DRAFT FULL PAPER YET.

This folder plans an empirical foundation for future legislative-mechanism simulation papers. It should not present the simulator as validated. The correct framing is benchmark construction, empirical boundary mapping, and validation readiness.

## Working Purpose

Build the empirical foundation needed for future simulation papers. This may become a data/resource paper if the data pipeline is expanded beyond the current flow sanity checks and missing-source map.

## Primary Question

What empirical signals can be assembled to calibrate, sanity-check, or validate legislative simulation models, and where do major gaps remain?

## Potential Paper Angle

Empirical Boundary Conditions for Legislative Mechanism Simulation

## Current Readiness

Not ready for a full paper draft.

Current repository evidence:

- `reports/empirical-validation-readiness.md`: 6 / 12 optional raw empirical inputs are ready.
- `reports/calibration-baseline.md`: 7 / 7 conventional-baseline flow screening checks pass.
- `reports/empirical-bridge.md`: empirical flow sanity checks map raw summaries to simulator proxies.
- `reports/empirical-validation-gap-report.md`: public support, campaign finance, implementation feedback, and law revision are high-priority missing areas.

Those artifacts are useful, but they do not validate generated public benefit, public support, revision moderation, harm, capture, administrative cost, correction over time, or mechanism rankings.

## Required Output Files

- `data-source-map.md`: empirical source inventory and current readiness.
- `metric-mapping.md`: empirical signals mapped to simulator metrics and boundary categories.
- `validation-boundary-table.md`: claim boundary table, readiness scorecard, and missing-data roadmap.
- `pipeline-plan.md`: data pipeline and reproducibility plan.
- `paper-plan.md`: paper decision, contribution boundary, and go/no-go.
- `draft-outline.md`: outline only; no full manuscript.

Existing supporting files in this folder may remain as supplemental planning notes.
