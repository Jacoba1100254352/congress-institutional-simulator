# Related Work Targets

This file lists literature areas to verify before drafting. It is not a completed bibliography.

- Agenda manipulation.
- Clone alternatives and decoy effects.
- Strategic voting and amendment sequencing.
- Poison-pill amendments and strategic agenda setting.

## Adversarial Governance and Participation

- Astroturfing and participatory-process manipulation.
- Bad-faith claims in grievance or objection systems.

## Simulation and Robustness

- Adversarial stress testing in social simulation.
- Worst-case analysis versus average-case reporting.
- Ablation analysis in agent-based models.
- Sensitivity analysis and uncertainty reporting.

## Legislative and Institutional Theory

- Committee gatekeeping under strategic actors.
- Organized interest influence.
- Veto gates and obstruction.
- Review, appeal, and correction systems.

## Citation Rules for the Draft

- Do not cite current stress probes as evidence of real-world manipulation rates.
- Separate normative claims about abuse from empirical claims about observed abuse.
- Verify every attack analogy before using it in the paper.
